About Us

At Lumière, we are more than just a restaurant; we are a constellation of culinary passion, dedicated to illuminating the artistry of fine dining. Our journey began with a shared vision: to create a space where exceptional ingredients meet innovative techniques, resulting in an unforgettable dining experience.

We believe in fostering a connection with the land and its bounty. That's why we work closely with local farmers and purveyors who share our commitment to sustainable practices. Our menu reflects the changing seasons, showcasing the freshest ingredients at their peak. This ensures that each visit to Lumière offers a new and exciting culinary adventure.

Our philosophy extends beyond the plate. Lumière's ambiance is designed to transport you, creating a warm and inviting space where you can relax, connect with loved ones, and savor the magic of a truly exceptional meal. From the elegant décor to the impeccable service, every detail is meticulously considered to enhance your dining experience.

We invite you to embark on a culinary voyage with us at Lumière. Explore our ever-evolving menu, indulge in the artistry of our dishes, and experience the warmth and elegance of our atmosphere. Let us show you how a meal can be more than sustenance; it can be a journey of discovery, a celebration of the senses, and a memory that will linger long after the last bite.

Wagyu Beef Tenderloin

Melt-in-your-mouth Wagyu beef cooked to your desired doneness, accompanied by a decadent foie gras sauce, roasted fingerling potatoes, and seasonal vegetables.

Seared Chilean Sea Bass

Delicate sea bass seasoned with a citrusy herb crust, nestled on a bed of wilted greens and finished with a light beurre blanc sauce.

Truffle Risotto

Roasted Rack of Lamb

Succulent rack of lamb crusted with a blend of herbs and spices, served with a rosemary demi-glace and roasted root vegetables.

Chocolate Sphere

A decadent dark chocolate sphere filled with a rich chocolate mousse and a touch of raspberry coulis, served with a vanilla bean ice cream.
